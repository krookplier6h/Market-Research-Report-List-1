<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Marine Emission Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extractive CEMs</li><li>In-Situ CEMs</li><li>Portable CEMs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Marine Emission Sensors market includes three primary types: Extractive Continuous Emission Monitoring (CEMs), In-Situ CEMs, and Portable CEMs. Extractive CEMs sample emissions by drawing them into an analyzer, providing detailed and precise measurements. In-Situ CEMs directly measure emissions within the exhaust stream, offering real-time data without the need for sampling. Portable CEMs are versatile devices designed for field use, enabling emissions monitoring across various locations and conditions, facilitating compliance and environmental assessments in marine environments.</p></p>
