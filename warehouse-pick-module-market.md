<p><strong>The Warehouse Pick Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Carton Flow Pick Module</li><li>Pallet Flow Pick Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Warehouse Pick Module market encompasses various systems designed for efficient order picking. The Carton Flow Pick Module utilizes inclined shelves and dynamic rollers to facilitate the flow of cartons, allowing for easy access and rapid retrieval of items. In contrast, the Pallet Flow Pick Module employs a similar principle but is designed for larger palletized goods, utilizing gravity to move pallets along inclined tracks. Both modules enhance storage density and streamline logistics, catering to different operational needs in warehousing.</p></p>
