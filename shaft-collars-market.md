<p><p>The shaft collars market comprises various types, primarily clamping and set screw shaft collars. Clamping shaft collars utilize a screw mechanism to secure firmly without marring the shaft, providing a reliable grip. They are often used in applications requiring repeat adjustments. In contrast, set screw shaft collars feature a set screw that locks onto the shaft, offering a cost-effective solution for static applications. Both types cater to diverse industries, including manufacturing, robotics, and automotive, ensuring precise shaft positioning and support.</p></p>
