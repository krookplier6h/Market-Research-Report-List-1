<p><p>The dewatering pumps market comprises two primary types: submersible and non-submersible pumps. Submersible dewatering pumps are designed to be submerged in water, effectively removing liquid from flooded areas or construction sites. They are typically portable and efficient in delivering high flow rates. Non-submersible dewatering pumps, on the other hand, operate above the water's surface and use suction to draw out water, making them suitable for more extensive applications, such as municipal drainage and industrial operations.</p></p>
