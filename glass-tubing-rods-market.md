<p><p>The glass tubing and rods market features several types, including ordinary glass tubes, chemical glass tubes, and borosilicate glass tubes. Ordinary glass tubes are commonly used for general purposes and are less resistant to temperature fluctuations. Chemical glass tubes are designed to withstand harsh chemicals and offer better durability. Borosilicate glass tubes, known for their high thermal resistance and low thermal expansion, are ideal for laboratory applications and high-heat environments, making them a preferred choice in scientific and industrial settings.</p></p>
