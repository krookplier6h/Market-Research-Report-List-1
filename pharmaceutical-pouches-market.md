<p><p>The pharmaceutical pouches market comprises various materials, each offering distinct properties. Polyethylene pouches are flexible and moisture-resistant, ideal for packaging solid medications. Polyvinyl chloride pouches are durable and versatile but less environmentally friendly. Polypropylene pouches provide good barrier protection and are lightweight. Polyethylene terephthalate (PET) pouches exhibit excellent chemical resistance. Aluminum pouches offer superior protection against light and moisture, making them suitable for sensitive products. Coated paper pouches provide a balance of sturdiness and printability, while "Others" includes emerging materials for specialized applications.</p></p>
