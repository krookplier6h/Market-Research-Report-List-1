<p><p>Laser patterning equipment is categorized into two main types: batch type and continuous type. Batch type systems process multiple substrates simultaneously, making them ideal for smaller production runs and intricate designs that require high precision. In contrast, continuous type systems operate on a steady flow of materials, enabling high-speed production and efficiency for larger volumes. Each type serves different market needs, with batch systems focusing on flexibility and detailed work, while continuous systems prioritize throughput and scalability in manufacturing.</p></p>
