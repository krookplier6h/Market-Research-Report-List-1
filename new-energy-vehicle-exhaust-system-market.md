<p><p>The New Energy Vehicle (NEV) exhaust system market features various configurations, primarily the single and dual exhaust systems. A single exhaust system utilizes one outlet for exhaust gases, simplifying design and reducing weight, making it suitable for compact vehicles. Conversely, the dual exhaust system employs two outlets, enhancing performance by increasing gas flow and reducing back pressure, which benefits high-performance or larger vehicles. Both systems are integral to optimizing NEVs’ efficiency and emissions management, aligning with green transportation goals.</p></p>
