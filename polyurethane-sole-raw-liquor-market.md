<p><p>Polyurethane sole raw liquor encompasses two main types: polyester and polyether. Polyester-based polyurethane is known for its excellent abrasion resistance and durability, making it suitable for high-performance footwear. In contrast, polyether-based polyurethane offers better hydrolysis resistance and flexibility, making it ideal for various applications, especially in high-humidity environments. Both types are essential in manufacturing polyurethane soles, catering to different performance requirements and environmental conditions, thus driving demand in the footwear and industrial markets.</p></p>
