<p><strong>The Automotive Flasher Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electronic Flasher</li><li>Thermal Flasher</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive flasher module market is primarily divided into two types: electronic flashers and thermal flashers. Electronic flashers utilize solid-state technology for reliable performance, allowing for precise timing and minimal power consumption. They are often favored for their compact design and compatibility with modern vehicles. In contrast, thermal flashers rely on a bi-metallic strip that bends with heat to create a delayed signal, resulting in a traditional, simpler design. Each type serves distinct applications based on vehicle requirements and performance specifications.</p></p>
