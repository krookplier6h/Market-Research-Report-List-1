<p><p>The planting robot market is categorized into two main types: Rear Tow Seeder Robots and Front Mounted Robots. Rear Tow Seeder Robots are designed to be hitched at the back of a tractor, allowing efficient sowing of seeds while optimizing soil coverage. In contrast, Front Mounted Robots attach to the front of the tractor, providing enhanced visibility and maneuverability, particularly in tight spaces. Both types are aimed at increasing planting efficiency and precision in modern agricultural practices.</p></p>
