<p><p>The hygiene converting machine market is categorized into full-servo and semi-servo types. Full-servo machines utilize advanced servo technology for precise control and high-speed operations, offering enhanced efficiency and flexibility in manufacturing hygiene products. In contrast, semi-servo machines combine servo and mechanical systems, providing a balance between cost-effectiveness and functionality. While full-servo machines are ideal for high-volume production and complex applications, semi-servo machines cater to businesses seeking reliable performance with lower initial investment and operational costs.</p></p>
