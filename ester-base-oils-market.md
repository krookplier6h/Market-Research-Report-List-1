<p><ul><li>Monoesters</li><li>Diesters</li><li>Polyol Esters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ester base oils are derived from the reaction of acids and alcohols, serving as high-performance lubricants. Monoesters consist of single fatty acid chains, offering excellent lubricating properties and biodegradability. Diesters are formed from two fatty acids, providing enhanced thermal stability and performance in demanding applications. Polyol esters involve multiple alcohols and acids, delivering superior oxidative stability and low-temperature performance, making them suitable for advanced applications in automotive and industrial machinery. Each type caters to specific needs within the lubricant market.</p></p>
