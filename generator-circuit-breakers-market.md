Vacuum Circuit Breakers operate in a vacuum environment, ensuring minimal energy loss and high reliability, often used in medium-voltage systems.</p><p>3. SF6 Circuit Breakers utilize sulfur hexafluoride gas for insulation and arc suppression, making them efficient for high-voltage applications with excellent performance in compact designs.</p></p>
