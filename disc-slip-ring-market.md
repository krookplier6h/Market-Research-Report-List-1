<p><p>The disc slip ring market is divided into two main types: Integrated Disc Slip Rings and Separate Disc Slip Rings. Integrated Disc Slip Rings combine the rotor and stator into a single unit, offering compact designs and simplified installation, making them ideal for space-constrained applications. In contrast, Separate Disc Slip Rings consist of individual rotor and stator components, allowing for greater customization and flexibility in various configurations, suited for more complex systems requiring tailored solutions. Both types serve critical roles in transmission of power and data in rotating machinery.</p></p>
