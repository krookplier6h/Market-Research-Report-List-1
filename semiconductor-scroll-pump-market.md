<p><strong>The Semiconductor Scroll Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static Vortex</li><li>Moving Vortex</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor scroll pump market is primarily divided into two types: static vortex and moving vortex pumps. Static vortex pumps utilize a stationary design to create a vortex, facilitating gas movement without moving parts, which enhances reliability and maintenance. In contrast, moving vortex pumps feature rotating components that generate airflow by manipulating the gas in a dynamic manner, providing efficient pumping rates. Both types serve crucial applications in semiconductor manufacturing, where precise vacuum conditions are essential for quality processes.</p></p>
