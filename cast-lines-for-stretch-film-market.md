<p><p>In the stretch film market, cast lines are categorized primarily into multi-layer and single-layer types. Multi-layer stretch films consist of multiple polymer layers, offering enhanced strength, clarity, and flexibility, which are ideal for demanding applications. These films provide superior protection and can effectively stretch without breaking. In contrast, single-layer stretch films are simpler in structure, typically made from one type of polymer. They are generally easier to produce, cost-effective, and suitable for lighter packaging needs. Both types serve distinct market applications.</p></p>
