<p><p>Fish farming equipment is categorized into three main market types: mariculture, coastal aquaculture, and freshwater aquaculture. Mariculture involves farming fish and shellfish in ocean habitats, utilizing equipment designed for saltwater conditions. Coastal aquaculture focuses on the cultivation of species near shorelines, often leveraging specific gear adapted for tidal zones. Freshwater aquaculture centers on breeding fish in inland water bodies such as rivers and lakes, requiring specialized equipment that accommodates freshwater environments, ensuring optimal growth and health of the aquatic species.</p></p>
