<p><ul><li>Fixed-installation projectors</li><li>Pico projectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The light projector market consists primarily of two types: fixed-installation projectors and pico projectors. Fixed-installation projectors are designed for permanent setups in venues like theaters, classrooms, and conference rooms, offering high brightness and superior image quality. In contrast, pico projectors are compact and portable devices, suitable for on-the-go presentations or personal use, often connecting to smartphones and tablets. Both types serve distinct purposes, catering to various user needs from professional environments to casual, mobile applications.</p></p>
