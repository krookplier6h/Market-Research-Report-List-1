<p><p>Crampons are essential for climbing and winter sports, categorized into three main types: hybrid, step-in, and strap-on. Hybrid crampons offer versatility, accommodating both automatic and semi-automatic systems, suited for various boot types. Step-in crampons feature a snug, secure fit, ideal for stiff-soled boots and preferred by advanced climbers. Strap-on crampons utilize adjustable straps, making them versatile for different footwear, and are popular among recreational climbers. Each type serves specific needs, enhancing safety and performance in icy conditions.</p></p>
