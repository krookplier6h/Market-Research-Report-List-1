<p><p>Automated Storage and Retrieval Systems (ASRS) optimize warehouse efficiency by automating inventory management. The Unitload AS/RS handles large goods using pallet-based storage for bulk items. Rack-supported building AS/RS integrates storage racks into the warehouse structure, maximizing vertical space while facilitating high-density storage. Miniload AS/RS focuses on smaller items, using specialized shuttles to retrieve and store products quickly in compact aisles. Together, these types enhance logistics, reducing labor costs and improving order accuracy across various industries.</p></p>
