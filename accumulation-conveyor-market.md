<p><ul><li>Zero Pressure Accumulation Conveyor</li><li>Low Pressure Accumulation Conveyor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Accumulation Conveyor market features two main types: Zero Pressure Accumulation Conveyors and Low Pressure Accumulation Conveyors. Zero Pressure Accumulation Conveyors ensure that items do not experience any pressure during accumulation, minimizing the risk of product damage and enabling smooth transitions. In contrast, Low Pressure Accumulation Conveyors apply slight pressure on products, allowing for efficient accumulation while maintaining control over item flow. Both types enhance material handling efficiency in various industries, optimizing workflow and reducing operational costs.</p></p>
