<p><p>The PF Phenol-methanal (Phenol-Formaldehyde) market is segmented into two main types: Novolac and Resol. Novolac resins are thermosetting and require a hardener to cure, making them suitable for applications in adhesives and coatings. Resol resins, on the other hand, are self-curing and offer quick processing, ideal for high-temperature applications like insulation and composite materials. Both types are widely used in various industries, including automotive, construction, and electronics, due to their durability and chemical resistance.</p></p>
