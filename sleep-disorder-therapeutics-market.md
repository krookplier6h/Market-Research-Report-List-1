<p><p>The Sleep Disorder Therapeutics Market is categorized into three primary types: insomnia, excessive sleep, and disturbed sleep. Insomnia focuses on treatments that help individuals who struggle to fall or stay asleep, often utilizing medications or cognitive therapies. Excessive sleep, or hypersomnia, targets individuals who experience prolonged sleep durations or excessive daytime sleepiness, addressing underlying conditions or lifestyle factors. Disturbed sleep encompasses therapies for those with disrupted sleep patterns, often involving lifestyle interventions and pharmacological solutions to promote healthier sleep cycles.</p></p>
