Additionally, the integration of digital technologies into ophthalmology practices enhances surgical precision and patient outcomes. <p><p>The intraocular lenses (IOL) market includes various types designed to enhance vision after cataract surgery. Multifocal intraocular lenses provide multiple focal points, allowing for improved distance and near vision. Adjustable intraocular lenses enable post-surgical adjustments for optimal focus, accommodating changes in vision over time. Aspheric intraocular lenses are engineered to reduce spherical aberrations, offering better contrast sensitivity and image quality. Together, these innovations cater to diverse patient needs, enhancing visual outcomes and quality of life.</p></p>
