<p><strong>The Enteral Feeding Bags Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pump Type</li><li>Gravity Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Enteral feeding bags are essential for delivering nutrition to patients unable to consume food orally. The market is segmented into two primary types: pump type and gravity type. Pump type bags utilize a mechanical pump to control the flow rate of the feeding solution, ensuring precision and reducing the risk of aspiration. Gravity type bags rely on gravitational force to administer nutrients, offering a simpler setup and lower cost. Both types serve distinct patient needs and clinical settings.</p></p>
