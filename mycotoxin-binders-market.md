<p><p>Mycotoxin binders are substances used to mitigate the harmful effects of mycotoxins, which are toxic compounds produced by molds that can contaminate food and feed. These binders work by adsorbing mycotoxins in the gastrointestinal tract, preventing their absorption into the body and thereby reducing health risks to animals and humans. The Mycotoxin Binders Market is experiencing significant growth due to rising awareness regarding food safety, increased regulations on food quality, and the growing prevalence of mycotoxin contamination in crops.</p><p>The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.6% during the forecast period, driven by the feed and food industries' need for mycotoxin management solutions. Additionally, the surge in livestock production and the expansion of the poultry and aquaculture sectors are propelling demand for effective mycotoxin control measures. Advancements in technology and innovation in binder formulations are also contributing to market expansion, making it a promising sector in the broader agricultural economy.</p></p>
