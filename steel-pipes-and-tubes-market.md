<p><strong>The Steel Pipes and Tubes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Seamless Tube</li><li>Welded Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steel pipes and tubes market is primarily categorized into seamless and welded types. Seamless tubes are created without any joints, providing higher strength and reliability, making them ideal for high-pressure applications. In contrast, welded tubes are formed by joining flat steel plates, allowing for easier manufacturing and cost efficiency, suited for lower-pressure uses. Both types serve diverse industries, including construction, automotive, and oil & gas, each catering to specific requirements based on strength, weight, and application.</p></p>
