<p><p>The Silicon-based Integrated Passive Devices (IPD) market is categorized into three main types: Digital & Mixed Signal IPD, RF IPD, and ESD/EMI Protection IPD. Digital & Mixed Signal IPD focus on integrating passive components for digital and analog circuits, enabling efficient signal processing. RF IPD serves radio frequency applications, enhancing performance in wireless communication. ESD/EMI Protection IPD are designed to safeguard sensitive electronic components from electrostatic discharge and electromagnetic interference, ensuring reliability and longevity in various electronic systems.</p></p>
