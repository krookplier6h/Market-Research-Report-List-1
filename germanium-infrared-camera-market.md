<p><ul><li>Short-Wavelength Infrared</li><li>Medium-Wavelength Infrared</li><li>Long-Wavelength Infrared</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Germanium infrared camera market is segmented into three types based on wavelength: Short-Wavelength Infrared (SWIR), Medium-Wavelength Infrared (MWIR), and Long-Wavelength Infrared (LWIR). SWIR cameras, operating between 0.9 to 1.7 microns, excel in low-light environments and are used for quality control in manufacturing. MWIR cameras, ranging from 3 to 5 microns, are ideal for thermal imaging in surveillance and military applications. LWIR cameras, from 8 to 15 microns, are widely used in thermal imaging for diagnostics and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
