<p><ul><li>Oil-based Soap Colorants</li><li>Water-based Soap Colorants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The soap colorants market primarily consists of oil-based and water-based options. Oil-based soap colorants, derived from natural or synthetic oils, offer rich hues and are ideal for cold-process soaps. They provide vibrant, long-lasting colors but may affect soap texture. Water-based soap colorants, on the other hand, are soluble in water and are typically used in melt-and-pour soap making. They are easier to use, highly versatile, and offer a more uniform color distribution, making them popular among crafters.</p></p>
