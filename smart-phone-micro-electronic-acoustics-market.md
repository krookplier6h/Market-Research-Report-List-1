Microphones capture sound for calls and voice commands, utilizing advanced technologies like MEMS for enhanced clarity. Speakers deliver audio output, providing users with high-quality sound for media playback and notifications. Receivers convert electrical signals into audible sound for phone calls, ensuring clear communication. Together, these elements enhance the overall audio experience in smartphones, driving demand for innovative acoustics solutions in the mobile industry.</p></p>
