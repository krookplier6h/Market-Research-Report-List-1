Semi-closed keys provide a balance, offering some protection while allowing visibility of the gears. Fully enclosed tuning keys are completely shielded, providing maximum protection from debris and environmental factors, enhancing durability and stability. Each type serves different preferences and requirements for musicians, influencing tuning stability and functionality.</p></p>
