<p><p>LCD Brightness Enhancement Films (BEF) are optical films used in displays to improve brightness and efficiency. The market includes various types: Normal Prism Sheets, which redirect light to enhance brightness; Multi-Functional Prism Sheets, combining brightness enhancement and anti-glare properties; Micro-Lens Films, offering precise light control for improved uniformity; and Reflective Polarizers (DBEF), which recycle unutilized light to boost brightness. These technologies collectively enhance display performance in devices like televisions, monitors, and smartphones.</p></p>
