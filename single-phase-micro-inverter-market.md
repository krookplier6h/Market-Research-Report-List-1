<p><p>The Single Phase Micro Inverter market is divided into two main types: Grid Single Phase Micro Inverters and Off Grid Single Phase Micro Inverters. Grid Single Phase Micro Inverters are designed to connect to the utility grid, converting solar energy into usable electricity while allowing for net metering and energy exchange. In contrast, Off Grid Single Phase Micro Inverters operate independently from the grid, providing power to standalone systems, ideal for remote locations or applications where grid access is unavailable.</p></p>
