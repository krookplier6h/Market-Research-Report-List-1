<p><p>Digital Risk Protection Platforms (DRPP) are classified into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based DRPPs leverage the scalability and flexibility of cloud computing, allowing organizations to manage and mitigate digital risks remotely with ease. They typically offer real-time monitoring, automated updates, and cost-effective deployment. In contrast, on-premise DRPPs are installed locally on an organization's infrastructure, providing greater control over data and security but requiring significant maintenance and upfront investment. Both types serve to enhance digital security strategies.</p></p>
