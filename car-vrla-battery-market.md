<p><p>The Car VRLA (Valved Regulated Lead Acid) battery market comprises mainly two types: AGM (Absorbent Glass Mat) and Gel batteries. AGM batteries utilize fiberglass mats to absorb electrolyte, offering high discharge rates and durability, making them ideal for start-stop vehicles. Gel batteries use a silica-based electrolyte, ensuring a slow discharge and enhanced safety, ideal for deep-cycle applications. Both types are maintenance-free, highly reliable, and widely used in various automotive applications for their efficient and long-lasting performance.</p></p>
