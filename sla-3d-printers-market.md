<p><p>SLA 3D printers utilize two primary market types: wax and polymer resin. Wax-based SLA printers are commonly used for creating intricate, high-resolution patterns primarily in investment casting and jewelry applications, allowing for detailed molds. On the other hand, polymer resin printers excel in producing functional prototypes and end-use parts, leveraging a range of photopolymer resins that offer diverse material properties. Both types deliver precision and detail, catering to various industrial and creative needs in the additive manufacturing landscape.</p></p>
